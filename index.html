Dilek Düştegör

Hi there, Dilek Düştegör here!

I am an Associate Professor of Applied AI for Cyber Physical Systems at the University of Groningen. My research focuses on digital twins and AI-based decision support for critical infrastructures such as water and energy systems. In parallel, I lead educational innovation initiatives that connect research, industry, and curriculum development across all levels of higher education.
